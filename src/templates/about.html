About

I'm Zachary — a developer who likes to understand how things work under the hood. I build from scratch, not because frameworks are bad, but because I want to know what they're doing for me before I let them do it.

This site and everything on it runs on a single VPS that I provisioned, hardened, and configured myself — from the TLS certificates to the Docker containers to the Nginx routing. Every project in the grid is something I built to solve a real problem or learn a real skill.

I believe the best way to learn is to build, break, debug, and repeat. The projects here are the proof of that process.
